Biography

My research interests mainly lie in Film PreViz and Virtual Production, especially in 3D Human Digitalization (i.e., Motion Capture, Real-time Animation, Modeling) and Cinematic Virtual Reality (i.e., Virtual Cinematography, Camera Motion Control). I am also interested in human-AI interaction solutions for computer vision and virtual reality.

I got my Ph.D. degree at Zhejiang University in 2019, working in the College of Computer Science and Technology, and majoring in Digital Art and Design, and got B.Eng. degree in Digital Media Technology at Shandong University in 2014. I spent rewarding time at National Centre for Computer Animation in 2017, Taiwan Tsing Hua University in 2013, and the University of California, Los Angeles in 2011.

I am looking for M.A. or MFA students to join our team, who are self-motivated and studying computer science, digital media technology, or related major with an engineering background. I hope you are good at programming and English, and highly interested in digital film technology. Don't hesitate to contact me via email, with your CV, transcripts, papers (if applicable), research proposal, and other representative materials you want present.
